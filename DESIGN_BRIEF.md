# Design Brief

## Style: Cinema Vault

The design should evoke a sense of exclusivity and sophistication, similar to a high-end movie theater. The layout should be elegant and refined, with rich colors and classic typography. The use of premium materials and subtle animations should create a luxurious atmosphere, perfect for entrepreneurs to connect and discuss movies. The blockchain verification element should be discreetly integrated, using subtle and elegant visual cues to convey security and trust.

## Design Tokens

| Aspect | Direction |
|--------|-----------|
| Colors | Rich golds and blacks |
| Typography | Classic and elegant serif fonts |
| Components | Sophisticated and rectangular buttons, cards, and inputs |
| Imagery | High-end photographs and illustrations |

## Theme Settings

- **Theme Color:** gold
- **Mode:** dark
- **Border Radius:** 0.3

## App Variant: FilmForge

> Connecting minds through cinema

FilmForge is a community-driven app that leverages blockchain verification to connect entrepreneurs and filmmakers through daily movie screenings and discussions. It combines the mindfulness of Headspace's daily practice with the community features of Discord, fostering a collaborative environment for creative problem-solving. By focusing on indie films and emerging artists, FilmForge promotes diversity and innovation in the film industry.

**Target Audience:** Independent filmmakers and entrepreneurs
**Monetization:** Subscription-based model with token rewards for contributors
**Complexity:** premium
